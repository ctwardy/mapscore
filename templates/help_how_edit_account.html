How do you alter your account information?

On your account menu, there is a link to edit your account. When you go to that page, your will be prompted with an account form with your current information filled in. Do not edit the fields that you wish to keep. Your current account photo will be kept even if you don't upload anything. To delete your current account photo, check off the box underneath the upload button. If you want to use a new account photo, upload a new photo to replace your current account photo. When you are finished updating your profile, enter your old password at the bottom of the form to verify all changes. You can also choose to delete an individual model or delete your entire account at any time.
