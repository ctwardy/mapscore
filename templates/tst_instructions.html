Test Instructions



The following set of procedures will allow you to sucessfully conduct a
test of your model. Please follow these procedures precicely in order
to allow our system to accurately rate your model.

A) For each test, you will be given 4 (lat,lon) points governing the boundry
of the search region. you will use these points in addition to the provided
information regarding number of horizatal and verticle cells to construct a
grid of square cells for your search region. Each cell must have the same height
and width. In order to verify that this vital step had been performed correctly,
for each test you must first pass an assessment where we provide you with 9 (lat,lon)
coordinates and you return the cooresponding gridcell of those coordinates. This will
ensure that both our and your interpretation of the test results are consistent.Grid
cells are identified in the following manner:





B) Your model will then rate each respective grid cell based on probability that
the lost person will be located in that cell. This process is left to you and your
individual model. You are encouraged to use all provided test case information as
well as all layers needed as input to your model

C) Once you have run your model and have developed a rating for each cell, you must
then construct a greyscale image representeing your result. Each pixel in your
greyscale image will represent a cell in your rated grid. For example, if your
grid has 'H' horizantal cells and 'V' vericle cells, you will return a greyscale
image with exactly 'H x V' pixels. In your greyscale image, the lighter the pixel
shade, the higher the probability that an individual is found in the cooresponding
grid cell. Thus, a shade of black representes low probability where a shade of white
represents high probability.

D) Lastly, you will be prompted to submitt your greyscale image for analysis
Once submitted, we will rate your image and close out the test case. You will
now only be able to view the case and can make no more modifications. The new
rating will be added to your model's overall rating and your leaderboard status
will be updated.
